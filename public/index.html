Searching tips:

  • Make sure to search while the match is live
  • Describe your event in less than 20 letters
  • Just use some keywords to best describe your match in 1-2 words
  • For eg: to search for a cricket match between India and Pakistan, search for "India Pakistan"
  • more eg: to watch a match between Roger Federer and Rafael Nadal, search for "Federer Nadal" or "Nadal Federer"
  • You might not get any result if you search for "Roger Rafael". Use their last name
  • Do not over elaborate. eg: "ICC cricket world cup India vs Pakistan"
  • There is no need of using event's name, eg ICC Cricket world cup, Roland Garros etc.
